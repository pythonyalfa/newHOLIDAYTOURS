About Us

Explore all tour of the world with us.

Welcome to Monument Valley Holiday Tours. I'm Steven Holiday, your seasoned guide and storyteller, born and bred in the heart of Monument Valley. As a proud member of the Diné community, my tours aren't just about ticking off landmarks; they're about weaving the threads of history, culture, and tradition into an unforgettable experience. I'm a friend eager to share my home with you. So come, let's wander the trails, swap stories, and discover the true essence of Monument Valley together. After all, in this land where time stands still, every moment is a treasure waiting to be unearthed.

  • Tour guide

    While the Monument Valley Navajo Tribal Park's vastness can be overwhelming, driving through alone might mean missing out on its hidden tales and wisdom. Welcome to the Monument Valley Holiday Tours, where every rock tells a story and every shadow whispers a tale of the land.

  • Friendly price

    As a Diné guide, I offer personalized, narrated tours for small groups of 1-7, ensuring a private exploration of our rich cultural heritage and breathtaking landscapes. I provide accessibility tours with a vehicle lower to the ground, catering to all travelers' needs.
